Data Structures And Algorithms

Overview

Data Structures are simply the study of various ways of organising data in a computer so that the information can be manipulated by systematic and step by step procedure called algorithms.

On the other hand algorithms are a sequence of instructions with a clear meaning executed with a finite amount of effort within a given period of time.
